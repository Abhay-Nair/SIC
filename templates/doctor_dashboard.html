LIST OF MEDICAL TESTS TO BE CONDUCTED:

  • KOH Prep Test : Test to detect fungal elements under a microscope for diagnosing fungal infections.
  • Rapid Antigen Test : A quick test that detects specific proteins of a virus or bacteria to confirm an active infection.
  • Wood Light Exam : Uses UV light to help detect skin infections or pigment changes.
  • Rapid Plasma Reagin (RPR) Test : A blood test that screens for syphilis.
  • Nasopharyngeal Swab Test : A test where a swab is taken from the nose to detect infection.

Health Guidelines

  • Ensure all medical tests are conducted in a sterile environment to prevent contamination.
  • Follow up with patients for any abnormal test results and provide necessary medical advice.
  • Maintain confidentiality of patient information at all times.
  • Stay updated with the latest medical protocols and guidelines for accurate diagnosis and treatment.
